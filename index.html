Cloud-as-Ouroboros:

The Post-Digital Infinite Scroll

Curated by Alan Perry, Snow Chen, and the Algorithm

The ouroboros coils through both our digital microcosm and celestial macrocosm: the Dragon’s Head and Tail above, the infinite scroll below. We consume and regurgitate content, feeding the system that feeds upon us. Within this pavilion, artists wield magick as compass and code, navigating the post-digital cosmos. In defiance of technofeudal lords, we dream in circuitry, move through shadow, and reclaim the ineffable from data’s devouring glare.
